Write Lyrics - Account Deletion Request

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Write Lyrics account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team
  2. Include the required information (see below)
  3. Confirm your request when you receive our response
  4. Wait for final deletion confirmation

📧 Contact Information

Email: support@writelyrics.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request - Write Lyrics

Required Information in Your Email

To process your request, please include the following information:

  • ✅ Account email: The email address registered with Write Lyrics
  • ✅ Confirmation: Write "I confirm that I want to delete my account"
  • ✅ Reason (optional): Help us improve by telling us why you're leaving

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • 🗑️ Account information: Email, name, app settings
  • 🗑️ Created content: Saved lyrics, ideas, worksheets
  • 🗑️ Usage data: App activity history
  • 🗑️ Subscription: Will be automatically cancelled

Data That Will Be Retained

For legal and billing requirements, some data is retained for limited periods:

  • 📄 Transaction records: 7 years (required by law)
  • 📄 Billing data: Per Google Play policies
  • 📄 Security logs: Maximum 30 days

Processing Time

⏱️ Initial response: 1-3 business days

⏱️ Complete deletion: 7-14 business days

Alternatives to Account Deletion

Before deleting your account, consider these options:

  • 🔄 Cancel subscription: Keep your account but cancel premium plan
  • ⏸️ Temporary pause: Stop using the app without deleting data
  • 📥 Export data: Download your lyrics before deletion

This page complies with Google Play Store policies and applicable data protection regulations.
