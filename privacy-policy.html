Write Lyrics - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information (email address, name) when you register
  • Song lyrics, ideas, and worksheets you create in the app
  • Usage data to improve app performance and features

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our songwriting services
  • Sync your content across devices (premium users only)
  • Send important account and service updates
  • Analyze app usage to enhance features

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Google OAuth: Optional login method
  • AdMob: Advertising for free users
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription management

Data Storage and Security

  • Free users: Data stored locally on your device
  • Premium users: Secure cloud storage with encryption
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Export your data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@writelyrics.app
